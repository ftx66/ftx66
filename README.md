## About Me / 关于我

- I am Freddy Fang (Fang Zhenghao), currently pursuing graduate studies in Data Science at City University of Macau. 我目前是澳门城市大学数据科学研究生。
- I work with [Minfeng Qi](https://github.com/Minfeng-Qi) on research topics around Agent Memory, Agent Security, and trustworthy agentic systems. 主要关注 Agent Memory、Agent Security 与更可靠的智能体系统。
- I enjoy turning research questions into prototypes, experiments, and clear technical narratives. 我喜欢把研究问题转化成原型、实验和可读性强的技术表达。
- My current interests sit at the intersection of memory mechanisms, tool-using agents, safety evaluation, and system design. 目前兴趣集中在记忆机制、工具调用型 Agent、安全评测与系统设计的交叉地带。
- I am open to thoughtful collaboration with people who care about reliable AI, open research, and practical building. 也欢迎围绕可信 AI、开放研究与实际落地展开交流合作。

## Research Focus / 研究方向

**Agent Memory**  
Designing memory mechanisms that help agents remember the right context at the right time.  
关注让智能体在合适的时机记住、取回并利用正确信息。

**Agent Security**  
Exploring safer behaviors, threat surfaces, and evaluation methods for agentic workflows.  
关注 Agent 工作流中的风险建模、防护策略与安全评测。

**Data-Driven AI Systems**  
Connecting research ideas with reproducible experiments, prototypes, and system thinking.  
关注把研究想法落到可复现实验、原型系统与工程实现。
